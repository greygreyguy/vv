Pony Play

As you may have gathered from the photos below, or possibly My reputation as a pony handler and trainer proceeds Me. I have been an equestrian and horse owner for some time, and am an expert in training and handling. I also have the uniquely comprehensive 'Pet Play Space' at Celestial Studios Derby, which has sandy manage, paddock, stable with rubber matting, shavings, hay and full tack, and show jumps. This enables Me to offer tutorials and demos for Dominants wishing to learn more about pony play, either as a human horse or as a handler. I offer shoulder riding, all fours schooling and upright pony training.
