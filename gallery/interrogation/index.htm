Interrogation

Again, there is no match for Me where interrogation is concerned. I went to stage school, and I spent much time as an adolescent watching and reading accounts of survivors from various wars, and their captors throughout the ages. I have a large holding interrogation cell and a padded cell which will be completed in April 2020, and I love nothing more than beginning the session at the door, dragging you to the cell, strapping you down to a chair, a hoist or the prison bed or toilet, and proceeding to use My various tactics of persuasion and perversion to embroil you in deep trouble with Governess. I have been known to torture for many hours and have some extremely unique ways of acquiring the information I need from My prisoners.
